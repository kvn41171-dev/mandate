Our Mandates

Mandates

Our core mandates as established under Section 4 of the Research, Science and Technology Act, 2004 (Act No. 23 of 2004).

1 Develop

We work with government to make rules and plans that guide how research, science, and technology happen in Namibia. Just like a country needs a development plan, we create the strategy that ensures research helps Namibia grow and compete globally.

2 Coordinate

We connect the dots between researchers. Universities, institutions, and scientists all do important work. We help them share information, avoid doing the same research twice, and focus on what Namibia needs most. Think of us as the bridge between great ideas.

3 Regulate

We build and maintain databases and systems that store all research done in Namibia. When someone wants to know what studies have been done, what was discovered, or who to contact, they come to us. We're like the national library for research.

4 Promote

When researchers discover something new, we help them turn that discovery into something useful - like a new medicine, a better farming method, or a technology business. We support entrepreneurs, help with patents, and connect inventors with people who can use their ideas.
